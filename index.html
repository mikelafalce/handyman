About

My name is Norbert Sienko, I am the owner of Chicago Handy. I’d like to take a few minutes of your time to tell you a little about myself and my skills, if you’ll allow me to share my journey with you. I am a hard-working individual, from Suwalki, Poland.
I began my career early, learning the fundamentals and basics working at my uncle’s development company. He taught me everything from how to read a tape measure, to professionally installing drywall, and painting. The kind of first job and family business that lots of kids start out with. I was picking up skills that have been passed down through generations, modernizations, various materials, operating different tools, and so much more!

I continue to be a hard-working, family man with roots in the community, which I share with my oh-so-supportive wife in the Bucktown Area of Chicago. We believe in community, in value, ethics, and safety, we won’t jeopardize our integrity by performing sub-standard work to your property. We provide personal services, by people you can trust.












Norbert Sienko

Master Craftsman

The skills of myself and any technicians I employ, are in conformity with local and state laws regarding certifications, apprentices, permits, and operate a fully licensed, bonded, and insured company.

And since we’re a local company, all of your taxes and related fees are staying local! That means more funding for schools, city repairs, and other programs that are funded through tax dollars. The benefit is three-fold by assisting you, you assist both our business and the community.


So, in carrying on my family-taught skills, Chicago Handy was a dream I found myself reaching for. After all, I had studied, improved, and mastered my craft to be in the position of breathing life into my dream establishing a home office, a headquarters, the birth of Chicago Handy in its earliest form.
